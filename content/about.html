I am a PhD student in Cornell’s Policy Analysis and Management program. I hold an MSc in Inequalities and Social Science from the London School of Economics and a BA in Psychology from the University of Winnipeg. Before starting at Cornell, I worked for two years as a data scientist and product manager at the people analytics firm Multi Health Systems Inc. I have experience in computational social science, product management, and team leadership.

I am passionate about a lot of things. Here is an abbreviated list in no particular order:

  • Computational social science

  • Travelling

  • Inclusive economic growth

  • Coding

  • The role of data in public policy
