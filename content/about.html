I am a PhD student in Cornell’s Policy Analysis and Management program. For the summer of 2021 I am working as a machine learning engineer at Graphika. I hold an MSc in Inequalities and Social Science from the London School of Economics and a BA in Psychology from the University of Winnipeg. I have also worked for two years as a data scientist and product manager at a people analytics firm called Multi Health Systems Inc.. In summer 2020 I served as a Data Science for Social Good Fellow at the University of Washington’s eScience Institute.

I am interested in a lot of things. Here is what I’ve thought about most lately:

  • Computational demography

  • Election fairness

  • Text as data

  • Software development

  • The need for stakeholder engagement in data science work
