I am a PhD student in the Cornell Brooks School of Public Policy. I hold an MSc in Inequalities and Social Science from the London School of Economics and a BA in Psychology from the University of Winnipeg. I am currently visiting the Max Planck Institute for Demographic Research (MPIDR) in Rostock, Germany, as part of a research incubator program. For the fall semester I am interning at Spotify.

I am interested in a lot of things. Here is what I’ve thought about most lately:

  • Computational demography

  • Using non-representative data to study populations

  • Machine learning for demographic measurement

  • Software development

  • The need for stakeholder engagement in data science work
